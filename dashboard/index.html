The whole loop, autonomous.

Signal → size → execute → settle. The inputs are live odds and on-chain state; the output is the real score. No human in the loop.

01 · read

Fair line

Demargined TxLINE 1X2 → the raw home-win probability. The draw stays in NO — no favourite-inflating renormalization.

02 · size

Quarter-Kelly

Edge = fair − market. Stake 0.25·f* of bankroll, capped 5% per position, 20% aggregate. Else hold.

03 · execute

Real USDC

Deposits Token-2022 USDC on the mispriced side, on its own deployed Solana program. Repeats until converged.

04 · settle

Trustless proof

A CPI to TxLINE’s validate_stat derives the winner from the on-chain Merkle proof — oracle pinned. Nobody types the outcome.

Quarter-Kelly sizing

Each bet is sized to a fraction of its edge, never a flat stake — a 5% per-position cap and a 20% aggregate exposure cap keep any single read from dominating the book.

f* = (p − q) / (1 − q) · stake = 0.25 · f* · bankroll

Kill-switch

Halts on a stale or malformed odds feed, or the instant the aggregate exposure cap is hit. It never trades blind, and it never trades unbounded.
