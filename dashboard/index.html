Quarter-Kelly sizing

Each bet is sized to a fraction of edge, not a flat stake — 5% per-position cap, 20% aggregate exposure cap.

f* = (p − q) / (1 − q) · stake = 0.25 · f* · bankroll

Kill-switch

Halts on a stale or malformed odds feed, or when the aggregate exposure cap is hit. It never trades blind or unbounded.
